Pav Bhaji

Pav Bhaji

🥘Ingredients

  • Potato, cauliflower, peas, capsicum
  • Butter, pav bhaji masala
  • Onion, tomato

    👨‍🍳 Steps

  1. Boil vegetables → mash them.
  2. Heat butter → sauté onions.
  3. Add tomatoes → cook till soft.
  4. Add pav bhaji masala → mix.
  5. Add mashed veggies → cook 10 min.
  6. Add butter + coriander.
  7. Toast pav in butter.
  8. Serve hot.
